# Screenshot Guide for Upwork Portfolio

## 📸 Screenshots to Capture

Open your browser to **http://localhost:3000** and capture these screenshots:

---

### 1. Hero Section (Main Landing)
**What to capture**: The full hero section showing:
- "Discover Your Next Favorite Media" headline
- Search bar with placeholder text
- Media type toggles (Anime, Movies, Manga)
- Genre filter badges
- "RAG-Powered AI Recommendations" badge

**Suggested filename**: `01_hero_section.png`

---

### 2. Trending Section
**What to capture**: Scroll down to show the "Top Rated Anime" section with:
- Anime cards with cover images
- Star ratings and type badges
- Hover effect on one card (shows synopsis preview)

**Suggested filename**: `02_trending_section.png`

---

### 3. Search Results
**What to capture**: 
1. Type a semantic query like: "sad romance anime with a bittersweet ending"
2. Click Search
3. Capture the results grid

- Large cover image

### 5. Recommendations View

### 6. Discover Mode

### 7. Watchlist (with items)

## 🎬 Video Demo Script (1-2 minutes)

1. **Intro** (5s): Show the landing page, highlight the modern UI
2. **Search Demo** (20s): Type a natural language query, show results
3. **Details & Recommend** (20s): Click a card, show modal, click "Find Similar"
4. **Recommendations** (15s): Show the AI-powered recommendations
5. **Discover** (15s): Click Discover, show curated categories
6. **Watchlist** (10s): Save items, show watchlist
7. **API** (10s): Quick flash of Swagger docs
8. **Outro** (5s): Back to hero with your name/branding

---

## 🖼️ Image Optimization Tips

1. **Resolution**: Capture at 1920x1080 or higher
2. **Format**: PNG for screenshots, WebP for web upload
3. **Crop**: Remove browser chrome if needed
4. **Annotate**: Use arrows/boxes to highlight features (optional)
